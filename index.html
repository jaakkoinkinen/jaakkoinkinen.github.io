About me

I was born in Hollola, Finland. From an early age I've been drawn to computers and multimedia. I'm a visually oriented person and I've also been drawing for as long as I can remember.

I studied digital media in Kymenlaakso University of Applied Sciences and graduated in 2015 as a Bachelor of Culture and Arts. Currently I live in Helsinki working as a web developer at Kaimana. My free time hobbies and interests include music, movies, video games and traveling.
